Tamales

Description

Tamales are a delicious traditional Mexican dish made from a corn based dough mixture. They are always filled with various things such as chili or meat, because of which there exits a lot of recipes for them. Some examples of different types of tamales include: rajas, mole, and green chili. Also corn, yes more corn.

This recipe will follow the most popular variant of tamales, red chili with pork. Nothing quite beats freshly steamed red chili tamale with a side of champurrado and bunuelos! Though as warning making tamales is a process, a very long process. Do not be discouraged though, it also can be fun and is well worth the effort!
