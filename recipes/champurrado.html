Champurrado

Description

So if you followed the last recipe you would have a bunch of delicious tamales but nothing to accompany it. Champurrado is chocolate drink commonly paired with tamales. It's a very simple drink made with milk, Mexican chocolate, masa harina, and cinnamon. The perfect warm drink paired with fresh tamales for the winter season. Comforting and sure to warm you up at any time of the day!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups milk
  • 2 ounces piloncillo, plus more to taste
  • 2 (3-ounce) discs Abuelita brand Mexican chocolate
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1/2 cup masa harina
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
